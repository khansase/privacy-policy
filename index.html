Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2025

Introduction

At Kestrel VPN, your security and confidentiality are at the core of all our efforts. Our mission is to ensure the privacy of our end users by providing best-in-class seamless VPN services. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information collected and how it is used.

Privacy and Data Protection

We are dedicated to protecting end-user privacy. Our core operations focus on a strict no-log policy; we do not collect, store, share, or transfer any data related to your online activities, browsing history, or personal data. This ensures your protected and guarded online presence at all times.

Collected Information

While we uphold a no-log policy, certain minimal information may be collected for service improvement and troubleshooting purposes:

  • Email address (for account management)
  • Payment information (handled by Apple Pay)
  • Basic application connection information (excluding browsing history and traffic data)

Safeguarding Underage Children

Our VPN services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from an underage individual, please contact our support to have the information removed.

Use of Information

We do not share, sell, or trade any user personal, private, or financial information with third parties. Any information collected is used solely for:

  • In-app feature enhancements and VPN service improvement
  • Account management
  • Customer support
  • Development of new features and services

Safeguarding Personal Information

We employ robust security measures to prevent information leakage. Disclosure of personal information is strictly prohibited, aligning with our core business values. Any changes to our Privacy Policy will be published on this page.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their accounts through the in-app menu. Upon deletion, no information will be retained on our end.

Policy Updates

Any updates to our Privacy Policy will be published on this page to inform our valued customers.

Contact Us

If you have any queries about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at support@kestrelvpn.com.
